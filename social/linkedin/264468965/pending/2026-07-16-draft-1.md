The company that built its reputation on password management is now tracking AI spend.

Why?

Because executives want teams to build faster with AI.

But that speed is creating a new kind of spending pressure.

Developers are consuming tokens at a pace that traditional budgets weren't built to manage.

IT and finance teams are being asked to forecast and justify AI investments without a clear view of what's actually driving costs.

Sound familiar?

Here's the reality:

Most companies have NO idea how much they're spending on AI tokens.

They don't know which team, which project, or which model is burning the budget.

Breaks it down by team, user, vendor, and model.

Sets limits. Sends alerts.

This isn't about slowing down AI adoption.

It's about making it sustainable.
