- **Target audience:** Business professionals, entrepreneurs, tech-savvy individuals seeking growth and insights

## Buyer Personas

*(Who specifically should feel spoken to by every post)*

- **Persona 1:** Enterprise Innovation Officer / CTO at 200+ person company (Financial Services, Healthcare, Retail) - Pain: Needs rapid AI solution development without vendor lock-in or heavy internal R&D
- **Persona 2:** CTO / Software Architect at 75+ person IT consultancy or agency - Pain: Needs to deliver AI-powered client projects faster with reusable components
- **Persona 3:** Founder / CEO / Head of Operations at 10-200 person SMB - Pain: Wants automation and AI competitiveness but lacks the engineering resources or budget

## Voice & Tone

- **Style:** Gary Vaynerchuk (Gary Vee) - Direct, energetic, bold, motivational, conversational. Short punchy lines, strong opinions, urgency. Powerful language with strong verbs, imperative statements, and intensifiers ("absolutely," "definitely," "100%"). Keep it real and direct with colloquial language. Create urgency with "right now," "immediately." Use repetition for emphasis. Casual but pointed tone.
- **Avoid:** Neutral reporting, corporate/formal tone, vague humble-brags, excessive emojis (max 0-3 per post), generic hashtags (#success, #motivation), salesy CTAs

## Hashtag Strategy

- Always use: 3-6 hashtags placed at the END of the post only
- Topic-specific: Industry, role, or topic-specific hashtags preferred
- Never use hashtags inside sentences

## Post Production Rules

*(Apply to EVERY post - non-negotiable)*

### Length & Structure
- Target: 900-1,300 characters (hard min 800, max 1,400)
- Max 14 lines total; 1-2 short sentences per line
- Structure: 1-3 hook lines -> 6-10 body lines -> 1-2 CTA lines

### Hook rules
- Pattern interrupt - ONE of: contrarian statement, direct challenge, consequence reveal
- Unicode bold sans for hook line ONLY - no Markdown, no *, no _

### Body rules
- ONE comment trigger: direct question, A vs B, or unfinished thought
- Contrast: old belief vs reality, OR short-term comfort vs long-term cost
- ONE save-worthy insight: a rule, checklist, or named pattern
- "you" and "your" throughout; ALL CAPS on 1-2 key words max
- Speak directly to a buyer persona's pain
- Gary Vee style ALWAYS takes precedence over any example post tone

### CTA rules
- Question, never a command; no selling language

### Hashtag rules
- 3-6 at END only; never inside sentences; prefer industry/role/topic-specific
- Avoid: #success, #motivation

### Formatting rules
- NO Markdown; Unicode emphasis on hook/key phrase only, never full paragraphs
- Emojis: 0-3 max per post

### News framing
- Reframe using ONE angle: "What this means for you" / "Why this is underestimated" / "The mistake most people will make" / "The second-order effect nobody mentions"
- Never neutral reporting - always add perspective, judgment, or implication
- If engagement feels weak after drafting - rewrite hook and CTA once before finalizing

## Post Style Guide

- Length: 900-1,300 characters
- CTA style: question-based
- Format: calibrate from feedback
